INTERACTIVE APPS · MULTIPLE DOMAINS

Shiny Apps

Explore game theory, statistics, AI/ML, and more through interactive Shiny applications. Each app has a paired tutorial explaining the underlying concepts.

Filter by domain

Apps

2x2 Nash Equilibrium Explorer

GT CORE · TIER 1

Enter any 2x2 payoff matrix and instantly see pure and mixed-strategy Nash equilibria, best-response correspondences, and expected payoffs.

Prisoner’s Dilemma Tournament

GT CORE · TIER 1

Run Axelrod-style round-robin tournaments with configurable strategies, noise, and population dynamics.

Spatial PD Lattice Visualizer

GT CORE · TIER 1

Watch cooperation and defection patterns emerge on a 2D lattice in real time.

Replicator Dynamics on RPS Simplex

GT CORE · TIER 1

Visualize replicator dynamics trajectories on the 3-strategy simplex.

Distribution Explorer

STATS & ML · TIER 1

Explore PDF, CDF, and quantile functions for all major probability distributions.

World Bank Country Comparison

PUBLIC APIS · TIER 1

Compare economic indicators across countries using live World Bank data.
